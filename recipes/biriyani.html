Recipe for Chicken Biriyani

Description

Along with the Nawabs of Bengal, Biriyani first came to the City of Joy, and as with all other things, Kolkata made Biriyani its own kind of specialty. Unlike Biriyanis from other places, you will find the Kolkata Biriyani with a reduced spiciness but blooming with flavour. As if the academically rich and sophisticated yet minimal culture of bengal is served in one dish. Indulge in the deliciousness of authentic Kolkata biryani with this recipe! and watch out for the Tasty potatoes in there.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Basmati rice or long grain dehradun rice
  • 1 kg 200 g chicken or murg(skinless biryani cut pieces)
  • 4 medium potatoes/aloo(cut into halves)
  • 3 medium to large onions(julienne cut)
  • 2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup tok doi or curd or yogurt
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  • 3 1/2 tsp biryani masala
  • 3 drops of rose essence
  • 1 1/2 tsp kewra water
  • 8-9 drops mitha attar
  • 1 pinch of saffron strand
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 2-3 inch cinnamon stick
  • 8-10 cloves
  • 10 green cardamom or elaichi
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 10-12 tbsp of ghee
  • 1/2 cup of refined oil
  • salt(as required)
  • 6-7 hard-boiled eggs(optional)
  • 50 gms bay leaves(for spreading on the bottom)
